Welcome to CURIOSITY!

CURIOSITY is a cybersecurity research group led by Prof. Zhenkai Liang at National University of Singapore.

Our research mainly focuses on system security, covering provenance, anomaly detection, trusted computing, Android malware detection, cyber range and attack replay, vulnerability assessment, program analysis, fuzzing, AI for security, etc.

Group Motto: Understanding systems, abstracting knowledge, connecting facts.
